2.7. Using Xlib With Threads

On systems that have threads, support may be provided to permit multiple threads to use Xlib concurrently. To initialize support for concurrent threads, use XInitThreads().

To lock a display across several Xlib calls, use XLockDisplay().

To unlock a display, use XUnlockDisplay().
